Some time ago, while sitting at the coffee, at the table next door were two men, in the 30s house, the coffee was poorly moved and I ended up starting to pay attention to their conversation.

One said he wanted only one woman, while the other emphasized that he liked to marry with more than one. The first unbeliever asks him: But why? Put up with a woman is it already as complicated the more we put up with several?

I was curious about the development of the conversation, as you might imagine… and especially with what would be the argument to defend is idea.

Of course, if you have more women all will have different abilities to solve different problems, which means you will worry much less…

I was amazed, not with the polygamous and monogamous perspective, but how it adapts to our abilities.

Have already thought of how people with monogamous skills usually always have an order to protect them, such as lawyers, doctors, etc. as people with skills do not have such protection? Examples? Entrepreneurs, Marketeers, programmers…

These people always need to be inside the last in the market! They are always learning and constantly innovating and testing!
